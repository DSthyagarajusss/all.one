    Sumadhura Capitol Towers

    Sumadhura Capitol Towers

    With the global IT revolution, Bangalore has developed into one of the most comprehensive office-leasing markets in India. Sumadhura Group, an ISO 9001-2015 certified organization introduce a one-of-a-kind landmark project. Sumadhura Capitol Towers designed by the world-renowned Morphogenesis is an utter, state-of-the-art commercial space in Whitefield, which is strategically located in Hope farm junction, with the upcoming metro connectivity to Whitefield, Kadugodi Metro station will be located right opposite to the project, ensuring effortless accessibility to the rest of Bangalore City.

    This Grade A+ commercial rental space embraces a fluidic architecture with carefully designed spaces to allow seamless movement. Each floor has a rectangular-shaped construction coupled with minimum columns allowing optimum daylight penetration across the floor and a 30% better flow of fresh air.

    Sumadhura Capitol Towers boasts of absolute efficiency in terms of floor plate design &energy consumption which in turn has resulted in LEED Gold Certification by the United States Green Building Council. We endeavor to create a workplace that is dynamic, vibrant, and full of life.
